Cha Cha's White Chicken Chili

Description

Cha Cha's chili with white beans and chicken is delicious but kind of spicy, so watch out!

    Ingredients:
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (4 ounce) can diced jalapeno peppers
  • 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chile peppers
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
    Steps
  1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium-low heat. Add onion; cook and stir until tender, 5 to 8 minutes
  2. Remove from the heat. Slowly stir in cheese until melted. Serve warm.
